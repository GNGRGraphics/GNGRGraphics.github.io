Who am I...?
GNGR is an accomplished digital media artist hailing from the United States, with a distinctive focus on character design and illustration. GNGR brings a wealth of creative expertise, drawing inspiration from a rich tapestry of influences that spans both personal experiences with prominent works in the entertainment industry and urban subculture. Starting their education career at Missouri State University, they have earned a Bachelor of Arts at the University of Missouri with a specialization in animation. GNGR is currently acting as Creative Director for the FSMG (Fulton Street Media Group) Internship Animation Team, as well as Project Manager for the production studio TinyAttic Productions.
GNGR’s artistic journey has been profoundly shaped by early interactions with digital media and street art, resulting in a body of work that seamlessly merges urban aesthetics with vibrant, whimsical energy. Drawing from the raw essence of urban subculture, GNGR’s creations not only showcase proficiency in the digital realm but also embody a rebellious ethos. Each piece is a deliberate exploration that challenges conventional artistic norms, inviting viewers into a captivating world where creativity reigns supreme. GNGR’s approach as an artist is characterized by a commitment to blending technical expertise with a rebellious spirit. Audiences are invited to embark on a visual journey that celebrates boundless creativity and imagination, while pushing the boundaries of artistic expression with professionalism and dedication.
